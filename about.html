Our Story

Bader Al Bidour is a trusted name in the automotive trading industry with over 21 years of management expertise in local and international vehicle markets. Built on a foundation of credibility and long-standing relationships, we specialize in delivering comprehensive automotive solutions that serve dealers, businesses, and individual clients across the region and beyond.

Our operations cover a wide spectrum of services including the trading of used and new vehicles for export, luxury automobiles, and specialized vehicles designed for secure transport. In addition, we provide a full range of genuine motor vehicle parts, accessories, and components through both wholesale and retail channels.

At Bader Al Bidour, we combine deep market knowledge with a customer-centric approach to ensure quality, transparency, and competitive value in every transaction. Our experienced team is committed to facilitating smooth trade processes, reliable logistics, and tailored solutions that meet the evolving demands of the automotive industry.

Mission

To deliver reliable and competitive automotive trading solutions by providing high-quality vehicles, parts, and services—supported by professional expertise, transparent dealings, and efficient global export capabilities that exceed customer expectations.

Vision

To be a leading regional and international automotive trading company recognized for trust, excellence, and innovation—connecting markets through premium vehicles and integrated mobility solutions while setting new standards in service quality.
